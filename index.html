León Ruben Joshua

Director/Musician/Programmer

León Siewert-Langhoff, born 1990 is a theatre maker and musician. He studied classical music in Berlin, Rostock and Paris and has been working as a musician in a variety of orchestras and ensembles from 2010-2014. Siewert-Langhoff has worked in different fields of theatre making since 2014 and as resident assistant director at Maxim Gorki Theater since 2016. 2018 he started directing his first own plays and works now as a freelance director in several theatres around germany.
